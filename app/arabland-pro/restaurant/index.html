Where food meets passion.

Founded in 2019, Saffron was born from a simple belief: every meal should be an experience. Our chefs blend classic techniques with bold innovation.

We source ingredients from local farms within 50 miles. From hand-rolled pasta to wood-fired breads, everything is made in-house daily.
