01Project Proposal

Most Used ▼

Send this to potential clients to outline your approach, timeline, and pricing.

PROJECT PROPOSAL
================

Prepared for: [Client Name]
Prepared by: [Your Name / Business]
Date: [Date]

---

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
[Brief description of what the client needs and why they reached out to you.]

2. SCOPE OF WORK
We will deliver the following:
- [Deliverable 1: e.g., Website redesign - 5 pages]
- [Deliverable 2: e.g., Mobile-responsive design]
- [Deliverable 3: e.g., SEO optimization]
- [Deliverable 4: e.g., Content migration]

Out of scope:
- [Item not included]
- [Item not included]

3. TIMELINE
| Phase          | Duration    | Deliverable              |
|----------------|-------------|--------------------------|
| Discovery      | Week 1      | Research & strategy doc  |
| Design         | Week 2-3    | Mockups & revisions      |
| Development    | Week 4-5    | Coded pages              |
| Testing/Launch | Week 6      | QA, launch, handover     |

4. INVESTMENT
| Item                    | Cost        |
|-------------------------|-------------|
| [Service 1]             | $[amount]   |
| [Service 2]             | $[amount]   |
| [Service 3]             | $[amount]   |
|-------------------------|-------------|
| Total                   | $[total]    |

Payment terms: 50% upfront, 50% on completion.

5. WHY US
- [Unique qualification 1]
- [Years of experience]
- [Relevant past project]

6. NEXT STEPS
1. Sign this proposal
2. Pay 50% deposit
3. Kick-off call scheduled within 48 hours

Accepted by: _______________  Date: ___________

02Freelance Contract

Essential ▼

Protect yourself and your client with a clear agreement.

FREELANCE SERVICE AGREEMENT
============================

This agreement is between:

Client: [Client Name], [Company], [Address]
Freelancer: [Your Name], [Business Name], [Address]

Effective Date: [Date]

---

1. SERVICES
The Freelancer agrees to provide the following services:
- [Service description]
- [Deliverables list]

2. TIMELINE
- Start date: [Date]
- End date: [Date]
- Milestones: [List key milestones]

3. COMPENSATION
- Total fee: $[Amount]
- Payment schedule:
  - 50% ($[amount]) due upon signing
  - 50% ($[amount]) due upon completion
- Late payment fee: 5% per month on overdue balance

4. REVISIONS
- [Number] rounds of revisions included
- Additional revisions billed at $[rate]/hour

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
- All work product transfers to Client upon full payment
- Freelancer retains the right to display work in portfolio

6. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to keep project details confidential.

7. TERMINATION
- Either party may terminate with [X] days written notice
- Client pays for all work completed up to termination date

8. LIABILITY
Freelancer's total liability shall not exceed the total fee paid.

Signatures:

Client: _______________  Date: ___________
Freelancer: _______________  Date: ___________

03Project Brief

▼

Send to clients before starting to gather all requirements in one place.

PROJECT BRIEF
=============

Project Name: [Name]
Client: [Client Name]
Date: [Date]
Prepared by: [Your Name]

---

1. BACKGROUND
[What does the client's business do? Who are their customers?]

2. OBJECTIVES
What should this project achieve?
- [Objective 1: e.g., Increase online sales by 20%]
- [Objective 2: e.g., Improve brand perception]
- [Objective 3: e.g., Launch in new market]

3. TARGET AUDIENCE
- Demographics: [Age, location, income]
- Psychographics: [Values, interests, pain points]
- Current behavior: [How do they currently solve this problem?]

4. COMPETITORS
| Competitor    | Strengths         | Weaknesses         |
|---------------|-------------------|--------------------|
| [Name 1]      | [What they do well]| [Where they fall short] |
| [Name 2]      | [Strength]        | [Weakness]         |
| [Name 3]      | [Strength]        | [Weakness]         |

5. SCOPE & DELIVERABLES
- [Deliverable 1]
- [Deliverable 2]
- [Deliverable 3]

6. BRAND GUIDELINES
- Colors: [Primary, Secondary]
- Fonts: [Heading font, Body font]
- Tone of voice: [Professional / Casual / Playful]
- Existing assets: [Logo, photos, copy]

7. TIMELINE & BUDGET
- Desired launch date: [Date]
- Budget range: $[min] - $[max]

8. SUCCESS METRICS
How will we measure success?
- [KPI 1: e.g., Conversion rate > 3%]
- [KPI 2: e.g., Page load time < 2s]

Send this to new clients to gather everything you need before starting.

================================

Welcome! Please fill out this form so we can get started.

- Industry:
- Main contact name:

- Phone:

ABOUT YOUR BUSINESS

- Analytics access? (Y/N)

COMMUNICATION

- Best time to reach you:
- How often do you want updates? (Daily / Weekly)
